About Us

This is the site for advertisement of events held in Pimpri Chinchwad college of Engineering located in nigdi,pune. In our college there are many technical as well as non technical events are arranged every year. Also one of biggest festival of Maharashtra is held in the month of feb-march every year in our college. In this festival many events are arranged in college on day and at night college annual gatharing where every year one celebrity come in college to interact with all student. Also there is not any entry fee to attend all the events and festival in our college.
